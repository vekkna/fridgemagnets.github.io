Fridge Magnets

Putting the shuffled lines of code into the right order is a good way of focusing on logic without having to worry about syntax.
This site turns blocks of code into shuffled and draggable "fridge magnets" which teachers can share with their students.

Enter your code here. Decide yourself what to do with curly braces etc. You can also add comments after lines if you want to give clues. Blank lines will be removed, as will white space from the start and end of lines.
