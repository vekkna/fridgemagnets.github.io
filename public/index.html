Fridge Magnets

Putting the shuffled lines of a program into the right order is a good way to learn the logic of programming without worrying about the syntax. This site turns a program into shuffled and draggable "fridge magnets" which teachers can share with their students via a generated code.

Enter your program here. Decide yourself what to do with curly braces etc. You can also add comments after lines if you want to give clues.
